# Site generation prompt for devcentr.org

This document provides the vision and content requirements for the official organization landing page for **Dev-Centr**.

---

## Context

- **Domain:** devcentr.org
- **Organization:** Dev-Centr
- **Mission:** Fundamentally improving developer productivity and workflow for pure human development and human-in-the-loop AI vibe-coding.
- **Category:** Pioneers of the **Development Orchestration Suite (DOS)**.

---

## Goals

- **Brand Hub:** Establish Dev-Centr as the central authority and laboratory for the DOS movement.
- **Visionary Design:** The site should feel high-tech, clean, and futuristic. Use glassmorphism, subtle gradients (red/charcoal), and premium typography.
- **Stratified Content:** Focus on the "Why" and the "Who". The philosophical shift from IDEs to Orchestrators.

---

## Prompt (copy-paste for design AI)

**Task:** Design and implement the official organization website for **Dev-Centr** at **devcentr.org**.

**Mission:** Fundamentally improving developer productivity for human and AI-assisted workflows.

**Core Concept:** We are the pioneers of the **Development Orchestration Suite (DOS)**. A DOS sits a layer above the code to manage the "gravity" of the modern developer's world.

**Requirements:**

1. **Visionary Landing:** A powerful "WOW" hero section that quickly conveys the mission: "Orchestrating the Future of Development."
2. **The DOS Movement:** Explain the shift from IDEs (editor-centric) to DOS (ecosystem-centric).
3. **Ecosystem Links:** Clear navigation to:
    - **devcentr.app**: The flagship Orchestration engine.
    - **docs.devcentr.org**: The centralized knowledge base.
    - **GitHub**: Our source-available ecosystem.
4. **Design Aesthetic:** Premium dark mode. Glassmorphism panels. Vibrant red accents (#FF0000 or similar adjusted for accessibility). Clean, tech-focused typography (e.g., Inter, Outfit, or Orbitron for accents).
5. **Tech:** SolidStart (Solid.js), TypeScript. Use the existing `src/app.tsx` and `src/app.css`.

**Content Pillars:**

- **Ecosystem Management:** Authoring and versioning environments as first-class citizens.
- **Visual DevEx:** Moving from file trees to system design blueprints.
- **AI Synergy:** Providing the deep context required for effective AI vibe-coding.
